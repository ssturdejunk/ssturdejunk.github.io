Local legacy

For nearly 30 years The Mountain Spoke has sold countless bikes and accessories to Bozeman native and visiting riders. But the most important thing we hope you leave our stores with is a love of cycling.
Sharing our cycling passion is a tradition that began with our founders when they bought a small bike shop on Valley Commons Drive in Downtown Bozeman. The little shop was one of only a handful of stores in Montana dedicated to mountain bikes. What they lacked in business experience our founders made up for in sheer exuberance for riding.
